Profile ^

I studied in three different languages through my primary (Turkish), secondary (English) and tertiary (German) education. That means I actually had all applicable classes in these languages, so I speak them more or less fluently. Even as a child I had a penchant for building things, which around middle school evolved into a passion for computing, and I went on to study business informatics, an interdisciplinary degree originating in Germany, where it's called "Wirtschaftsinformatik". It was during my studies that I first came into contact with SAP, by way of an internship as an ABAP developer.

After graduation and a few months in compulsory military service, I embarked on my SAP career as a consultant for the management accounting ("CO" for controlling) module. Within a couple of years, I took up the project system ("PS") module in addition. Having worked solely with consulting houses through these last 6+ years, I've seen my fair share of projects and support in a range of industries ranging from food to automotive. You will find below an inexhaustive list of projects I've worked on.
